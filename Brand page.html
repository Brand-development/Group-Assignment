About the brand

U-niQ's Mission

Our Tea mainly focusses on you as the customer. The problems you might be dealing with and how we can help with that.
Our tea and chocolate are made of homegrown organic ingredients. Moreover, we want our customers to stay motivated even though the stress and for that reason we added motivational quotes on the tea bags.
Everything we offer, from the ingredients to the packaging and the little gifts we offer is supposed to create a whole new, and relaxing experience. Something you do not want to miss!

We focus on you!
