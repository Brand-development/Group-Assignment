About the brand

U-niQ's Mission

Our Tea mainly focusses on you as the customer. The problems you might be dealing with and how we can help with that.
Our tea is made of homegrown organic ingredients. Moreover, we want our customers to stay motivated specially when they experience stress and for that reason we added motivational quotes on the tea bags.
Everything we offer, from the ingredients to the packaging and the little gifts we offer is supposed to create a whole new, and relaxing experience. Something you do not want to miss!

More than ever, families are prioritizing health and wellness, staying mindful of what goes in the food they eat. We're proud to maintain our commitment to better-for-you options for both kids and adults. From plastic straws to plastic lids, consumers are demanding more sustainable options. We are working hard to figure out packaging options across all of our products and to keep our stakeholders up to date on our progress and challenges.
In 2003, we began our Fair Trade journey, and today, we have paid more than $2 million to our supplier partners in Fair Trade premiums. These funds are used at the discretion of the farmers, who vote on how to spend the resources. The premiums have supported the purchase of vital equipment, health care services, and education. If it's not organic, it's not U-niQ. A key part of our mission is to make sure all families have access to organics by making our products widely available across the country.

We focus on you!
